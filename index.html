Hi, I'm Sebastien! King Arthur's Curse is my first game!
I love escape games, but wanted puzzles that feel natural, not far-fetched. I also craved more freedom and multiple endings.
I couldn't find it, so I made it.

See the button above? It's a quick and dirty demo I made for you. It's no match for the real thing, but it'll give you a feel for it.
My evil plan, of course, is that you end up liking it and buying the game. And who knows, you might even end up in the Hall of Honour!
